What do my results mean?

With this task we measure how much risk individuals are willing to take in the face of uncertainty. The number of times that you were willing to pump the balloon is a strong indicator of your general tolerance for risk. Your score is calculated only from balloons in which you chose to cash in your earnings, because popped balloons are not a representation of how many times you were willing to pump the balloon.

Why do we study risk with this task?

This task has previously been shown to strongly relate to risky behaviors in the real world. With this task we are able to identify both individuals who are especially risky as well as individuals who are especially sensitive to risk. Understanding how individuals engage in risky behaviors is critical for improving methods to help populations of individuals who make too many or too few risky decisions in their life.
